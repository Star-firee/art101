⭑☆⋆How to Make Dog Pancakes⭑☆⋆

  • Take an egg out of the fridge.
    • Carefully place the egg on the counter.
    • Close the fridge.
  • Get a banana and place it on the counter
    • Grab a knife and a cutting board.
    • Place the cutting board on the counter.
    • Peel the banana.
    • Throw the peel into the trash can.
    • Place the banana on top of the cutting board.
    • Cut it in half.
  • Place half of the banana into a medium sized bowl.
    • Grab a fork.
    • Use the fork to mush the banana until it is mashed completely.
  • Grab the egg.
    • Crack the egg open and place the egg into the bowl with the mashed banana.
    • Throw the shell of the egg into the trash.
  • Mix the egg and the mashed banana together until they are completely mixed together.
    • Once completely mixed, place the bowl on the counter with the fork in the bowl.
  • Grab a non-stick pan.
    • Place it on top of the stove.
  • Grab a spatula.
    • Place it on the counter.
  • Turn on the stove.
  • Grab the medium sized bowl with the egg-banana mixture and the spatula.
  • Place about two tablespoons of the mixture into the non-stick pan.
  • Wait a couple minutes for the pancake to cook.
    • When the pancake begins to form bubbles, flip the pancake using the spatula.
    • Wait a couple of minutes for the pancake to cook on the other side.
  • When both sides are brown and fully cooked, use the spatula to pick up the pancake.
  • Place the pancake onto a plate or a dog’s bowl
    • Add toppings like whipped cream, blueberries, strawberries, or the other half of the banana!
  • Serve to the dog!
